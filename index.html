About Hotel Gulati Suites

Located in the heart of Washim, Maharashtra, Hotel Gulati Suites offers a luxurious and warm retreat for travelers seeking comfort and elegance. Our hotel combines modern amenities with personalized service, ensuring every guest feels at home. Whether you're here for business or leisure, we strive to make your stay unforgettable.
